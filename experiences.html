Beyond the Suite

At Royal Masia, every experience is composed for you — tailored, unhurried, and genuinely memorable. Whether you seek stillness in our spa, adventure in our gardens, or the joy of creation in our kitchens, your personal butler will orchestrate it all.

The Masia Spa

Ancient Wisdom,
Artisan Touch

Our spa is not a facility — it is a destination within the destination. Drawing on ancient healing traditions from across the tropics, our therapists work with botanical elixirs, warm volcanic stones, and time-honoured techniques to restore balance and awaken the senses.

Each treatment begins with a 15-minute consultation to ensure your journey is precisely calibrated to your needs. Private spa suites, a flotation pool, and our botanical steam room await.
