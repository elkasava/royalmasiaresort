Dining at Royal Masia

Five Distinct Destinations

A Table Worth Remembering

Every meal at Royal Masia is a composition — of local ingredients coaxed to their finest, of service that anticipates rather than responds, and of settings that make every occasion feel like the only occasion that matters.

Fine Dining · French Colonial

The Verandah

Our signature restaurant occupies the original colonial verandah, where rattan chairs and plantation shutters frame a menu of contemporary cuisine rooted in local traditions. Chef Armand Leclerc sources daily from our kitchen garden and neighbouring farms, translating them into dishes of quiet elegance.

Open daily · Dinner 18:30–22:30 · Sunday Brunch 11:00–15:00

Bar & Lounge · Heritage Cocktails

The Colonial Bar

Unchanged since 1924, the Colonial Bar is the soul of Royal Masia. Sip a Long Masia Sling — our answer to Singapore's famous original — beneath slowly revolving ceiling fans, or choose from a cellar of aged Armagnacs and rare tropical rums that few bars in the world can match.

Open daily · 11:00–01:00 · Happy Hour 17:00–19:00
View the Menu
Outdoor Dining · Tropical Cuisine

The Spice Garden Terrace

Dine beneath a canopy of fairy lights and jungle palms at The Spice Garden Terrace, where our chefs interpret the bold flavours of the region in a relaxed, convivial setting. The weekly chef's table — hosted in the working herb garden itself — is among our most celebrated experiences.

Open Tuesday–Sunday · Dinner 18:00–22:00

Breakfast & Brunch · The Long Table

The Long Table

Begin your day at The Long Table — a generous celebration of morning. The estate kitchen produces everything from warm pastries and tropical fruit selections to made-to-order egg dishes and a changing plate from our wood-fired oven. It is a meal that reminds you: today is yours entirely.

Open daily · 07:00–11:00
